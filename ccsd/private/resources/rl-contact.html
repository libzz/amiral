Rural Link


Rural Link provides complete wireless networking solutions that can be used for internal business connections and Internet connectivity. Developed especially for rural communities over four years by the WAND network research group at the University of Waikato, Rural Link solutions can be installed without any background in telecommunications networking. They have low power requirements, and can be used to send high-speed data over substantial distances.

Please visit our website at: http://www.rurallink.co.nz/
